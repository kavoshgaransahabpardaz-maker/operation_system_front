Section 3 — The Core Engine
Purpose: the trust wedge — why we're different from the AI-hype tools.
Headline: Flag, don't guess. Legal logic built by lawyers — not guessed by a machine.
Body: Unlike generic "AI-powered" tools that guess, Veritariff runs on deterministic, expert-validated legal logic. The engine reads and organises your documents — but every output is cited to specific legal text, so you're always ready for post-Brexit divergence, CBAM, EUDR and sanctions. We give your experts a decision they can validate in seconds, instead of hours of manual research.
Three proof points (icons + one line each):
Cited, not a black box — every answer shows its receipts (the legal source)
Expert-validated — reviewed by customs specialists; sharper with every determination
Your data stays yours — we never train on it or sell it
Small line: the engine reads your documents; it never makes the legal call.

Section 4 — Results
Purpose: concrete proof of value.
Headline: Trade made easy. Made certain. Made fun.
Four stat blocks:
~90% faster — a 2-hour manual declaration becomes an 8–15 minute workflow
Scale without headcount — automate the routine 95%; the same team handles far more
Bankable compliance rating (roadmap) — a verified shipment history banks and insurers can underwrite; compliance becomes a financial asset
Audited chemistry — the only platform that proves preferential origin by reading the Mill-Test-Certificate (melt & pour) under rules like TCA ORIG.19
PM note — substantiate before launch: the "90% / 8–15 min" and "scale without headcount" numbers are our target claims; mark them as based on the workflow design until we have a customer figure, and label "bankable compliance rating" as roadmap. Honesty is the brand — no unproven hard numbers stated as fact.

Section 5 — TradeWatch
Headline: Get ahead of the news — and out of the panic.
Sub: TradeWatch tells you what every change actually does to your shipment — in plain English, before it becomes a problem. It watches the noise so you don't have to:
News effect — a new tariff, sanction or rule, and exactly what it means for your duty and origin
CDS release effect — when HMRC changes CDS, what it does to your declarations
CDS error explainer — paste a rejection, get the plain-English cause and the fix
HMRC availability monitor — is the system down, or is it you? Know instantly
